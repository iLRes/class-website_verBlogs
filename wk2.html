Designer Gleb's geometric design on the home page struck my interest; diamond shapes of grey and black, side by side, as if they were a cluster of boxes. When the mouse cursor hovers over the links to other pages, nothing in particular happens. However, the images in the center obtain a grey filter over them. Visual display is pleasing to look at, not overly bright.
