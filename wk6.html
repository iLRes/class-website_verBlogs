Home

I found Plane to be entertaining. When I tried to scroll the page down at first, I though something was amiss. The "something amiss" was the interactive title on the home page; it grew larger the more I tried to scroll downward, and then the hyphen between the two words in the title turned into a 3D sculpture. A highly interactive site is a fun site, in my opinion.
